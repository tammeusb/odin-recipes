Smoothie

Description

This recipe is so darn good. It's so simple, too.

I make this smoothie every morning as soon as the sun rises. It's just that good.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Fruit (berries work best)
  • Cashew Yogurt
  • Hard-Boiled Egg (with shell still on!)
  • Kefir
  • Filtered Water
  • Oats (optional)

Steps

  1. Dump ingredients into smoothie cup
  2. Run smoothie for a long time @ 7 AM
  3. Wake up all of your roommates or neighbors
  4. Enjoy!
